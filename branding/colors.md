# Soteria Guardian Colors

The official palette will be finalized from the approved logo assets.

Initial working roles:
- Guardian Navy — primary headings, trust, stability
- Soteria Teal — secondary brand color, care, technology
- Torch Gold — highlights, alerts, protection
- Laurel Green — wellness and environmental status
- Warm Ivory — light backgrounds
- Charcoal — dark-mode surfaces and body text

Exact HEX, RGB, CMYK, and accessibility contrast values will be added after final asset sampling and review.
