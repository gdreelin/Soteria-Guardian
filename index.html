WHY SOTERIA GUARDIAN

Protection without sacrificing independence

Soteria Guardian combines smart-home technology, deterministic safety rules, caregiver tools, and optional intelligence into one cohesive platform.

🚨

Emergency SOS

Wearable, fixed-button, app, and sensor-triggered emergency workflows with acknowledgment and escalation.

📷

Camera Verification

Fast access to live camera status, snapshots, and event context during active alerts.

📱

Caregiver Dashboard

Resident location, last movement, device health, battery state, and alerts in one accessible view.

🧠

Wellness Insights

Routine monitoring, inactivity checks, trend summaries, and future AI-assisted event correlation.

💡

Accessible Alerts

Visual light patterns and tactile controls for residents who may not hear voice prompts reliably.

🔒

Privacy First

Local automations, least-privileged access, secure credentials, and explicit stale-data indicators.
